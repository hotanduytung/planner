Planify Help & Guide

Welcome to Planify Enterprise, a complete clone of Microsoft Planner featuring Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

🔑 Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) Matrix:

  • CEO/Admin (Alice): Full permissions across all projects and tasks. Can add/remove members, create projects, and edit all tasks.
  • Project Manager (Bob): Can create projects and has full edit access to tasks in projects they belong to.
  • Team Member (Charlie): Can drag and update status/checklist on tasks assigned to them. Can write comments. Cannot edit overall task titles or delete tasks.
  • Viewer/Client (Diana): Read-only access to Kanban Board, Grid View, and Dashboard. Cannot make any changes.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • View Modes: Click the Board / Grid toggle at the top right of the Kanban page to switch layout views.
  • Checklists: Open a task details modal to interactively manage checklists. A progress bar will show on the Kanban card.
  • Delays: Incomplete tasks that past their deadline are flagged as Delayed and marked in red automatically.
